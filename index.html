How It Works
Three ways to get involved
Whether you build tools, use them, or partner with us — there's a place for you on CivicByte.
1

Build

Develop a civic tech tool that solves a real community problem. Use any stack — we care about impact, not frameworks.

2

Submit

Submit your tool to CivicByte through our GitHub-based process. We review for quality, accessibility, and community value.

3

Impact

Your tool gets listed on the platform, discoverable by communities, advocates, and organizations who need it most.
