Privacy Policy

CivicByte is built on a simple principle: public data should serve the public, not the other way around. We don't collect your personal information.

TL;DR

CivicByte does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. The app and website are free tools — you are not the product. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information from users of the CivicByte app or website.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Cookies or tracking pixels
  • Any form of account or login data (no accounts exist)

2. How the App Works

The CivicByte iOS app is a curated directory of free civic tech tools. When you tap a tool, the app opens it in an in-app browser (WebView) pointed directly at the tool's URL. All data processing happens on the tool's own server or in your browser — not through CivicByte.

We do not intercept, log, or store any data you enter or retrieve while using tools within the app.

3. Third-Party Tools and Services

CivicByte links to civic tools built and maintained by third-party developers. These tools are independent services and have their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any tool you use.

Current tools on the platform include:

  • NYC School Budget Tracker — powered by NYC Open Data
  • NYC Building Report — powered by NYC HPD Open Data
  • NYC Landlord Watch — powered by NYC Open Data

None of these tools require an account or collect personal data as part of CivicByte's platform.

4. Open Source

All CivicByte code is open source under the MIT License. You can inspect exactly what the app does at any time:

  • Website: github.com/civicbytedev/civicbytedev.github.io
  • iOS App: github.com/civicbytedev

5. Children's Privacy

CivicByte does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, we comply with COPPA by design.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version at this URL with a revised "last updated" date. Given that we collect no data, material changes are unlikely.
