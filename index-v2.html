§01 — What we stand for

Five rules we don't break.

The decisions that made CivicByte what it is — and keep it from becoming something it shouldn't be.
01

Free forever.

No paywalls, no freemium tiers, no accounts. If you have an address, you get the data. That's the deal.

02

Open source.

MIT-licensed, public repos, documented data sources. Anyone can fork, audit, translate, or improve the work.

03

Real data only.

Every result comes directly from NYC Open Data — HPD, DOB, 311, the courts. No scraping, no estimates, no guessing.

04

Works for everyone.

Plain language, mobile-first, no jargon. If your grandmother can't use it on her phone, it isn't finished.

05

Accuracy over speed.

We'd rather not ship than ship something wrong. Civic tools have real consequences for real people.

—

One we bend sometimes.

Adding new tools takes time. We'd rather be slow and thorough than fast and sloppy.
