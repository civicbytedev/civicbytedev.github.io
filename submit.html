Review process
  1. 01 A person reads it Every submission is read end-to-end by a member of the editorial board. No keyword filters, no auto-replies, no triage queues you can't see. Day 1–2 ·
  2. 02 We try the tool On a real device, with a real address or query. Timed against the four standards. Notes filed back if anything blocks a non-technical user. Day 3–5 ·
  3. 03 A decision, in writing Listed, listed-with-revisions, or not-yet — with specific reasons either way, and an open invitation to resubmit. By day 7 ·
