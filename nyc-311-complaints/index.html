Common Complaint Types
Noise
Residential, street, commercial — one of the most common 311 categories in NYC.
Heat / Hot Water
Legally required year-round. Landlords must provide heat Oct–May; hot water 365 days.
Plumbing & Water
Leaks, flooding, no water — routed to DEP or HPD depending on the issue.
Pests / Rodents
Routed to DOHMH. Buildings with repeat complaints may face increased inspections.
What to do with this data
  • 1Check your building's complaint history before signing a lease to spot recurring problems.
  • 2File your own 311 complaint at portal.311.nyc.gov or by calling 311.
  • 3Cross-reference with HPD violations for a full picture of building conditions.
  • 4Organize with neighbors — repeated complaints help establish patterns for legal action.
