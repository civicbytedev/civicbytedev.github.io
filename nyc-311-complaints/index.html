How it works

Spot patterns that matter.

01

Enter the address

Street and borough. We query the full NYC 311 dataset — 35M+ records — live from your browser.

02

Read the history

Complaint type, agency, date, and resolution for every filing. See what keeps coming up.

03

Use the pattern

A building with 40 heat complaints in two winters tells a story. Bring the data to a hearing, a lease negotiation, or a neighbor.

Where the data comes from

All public. All cited.

Primary

NYC 311 Service Requests

NYC Mayor's Office of Operations. All 311 requests since 2010, updated daily via NYC Open Data (erm2-nwe9).

API

NYC Open Data · Socrata

All queries are made live from your browser. No middleman, no caching.

Coverage

100+ Complaint Types

Heat, hot water, noise, rodents, plumbing, sanitation, street conditions, and more — all filterable.

Agencies

HPD · DOHMH · NYPD · DOT · DSNY

Each complaint is routed to the responsible agency. Resolution notes appear when available.
