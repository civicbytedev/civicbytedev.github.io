CivicByte/ Tools/ NYC Landlord Watch
Demo mode Housing · Accountability

One search. A landlord's entire record.

Portfolio-wide violation history for any NYC landlord.

Type a landlord or LLC name to pull every property they own across NYC, the total violations on file, and a risk score that surfaces chronic offenders. Built for tenants, organizers, and reporters who need the whole picture, not one building.

Demo mode. Resolving LLCs back to their parent landlords requires an ACRIS-deed pipeline we're still building. While that lands, this tool returns sample portfolios for three known landlords — search any of them or try your own name to see the format.

How it works

Follow the whole portfolio.

01

Search a name

Type a landlord, LLC, or managing agent. We fuzzy-match across registration filings and known shell companies.

02

See the portfolio

Every building they own, how many units, and the full violation tally. Sorted by severity, not just count.

03

Cross-reference

Jump from any building straight to its Building Report or 311 history. Every finding is traceable to the source.

Where the data comes from

All public. All cited.

Primary · Ownership

HPD Registration Contacts

Owner, head officer, and managing agent filings. Resolves LLC chains to the people behind them where records allow.

Primary · Violations

HPD Maintenance Code Violations

Every logged HPD violation, severity class, and status. Used to compute portfolio-wide risk scoring.

Secondary · Deeds

ACRIS · Real Property Filings

NYC Department of Finance. Deed transfers and mortgage filings used to confirm ownership across LLCs.

Reference

PLUTO · Land Use Records

Used to resolve BBL identifiers and unit counts across a landlord's portfolio.
